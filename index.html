Us.

The Beginning

My partner and I met in August of 2022. We decided to meet up in person after having messaged on the dating app Hinge for a couple of weeks. Our conversations on Hinge had come so naturally, it was a pleasant change. We hit it off, bonding over our mutual nerdiness and devotion to the fandoms we loved.

The Guy

Christian grew up in a rural Missouri town after his family moved there from Houston, Texas. He’s got a big loving family that has welcomed me in warmly. He is a fantastic uncle to his many nieces and nephews. He and his brother-in-law run a business called Beskar Books, selling comic books. He enjoys playing music, and has a beautiful voice. He is also quite skilled at video games--in particular, a game called Destiny.

The Couple

Our relationship has been incredibly rewarding thus far. We grow and learn together, and it’s wonderful to feel safe and secure in doing so. Though we have many hobbies and activities we enjoy, probably our favorite thing to do is to stay in and watch movies, tv, and/or youtube. We are especially fond of the Corridor Crew youtube channel, and look forward to watching the latest video on Saturday mornings. Outside of that we enjoy watching sports, and go to games when we can. Now that football season is over, we have transitioned into baseball. We try to travel when we can squeeze a quick trip in and have fun exploring new places together.

The Future

Our relationship is still new, but we work hard on communication and showing up for each other. That has played a huge role in our happiness. I’m looking forward to what the future holds for us. We are planning to move into a place with my best friend, Cecile, in a couple of months. I am especially looking forward to paddle boarding and camping trips this summer. I am grateful on a daily basis for our relationship, and I am very happy that we met at this time in our lives.
